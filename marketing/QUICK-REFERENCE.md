## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Don't**:
- Over-promise and under-deliver
- Ignore negative feedback
- Be salesy (focus on value)
- Post without testing
- Forget to engage in comments
- Launch on Friday (bad PH timing)
- Neglect international audience

✅ **Do**:
- Be transparent about limitations
- Respond quickly to criticism
- Show real examples
- Test all links before posting
- Reply to every comment
- Launch Wednesday 12:01 AM PST
- Support Chinese community (huge market)
